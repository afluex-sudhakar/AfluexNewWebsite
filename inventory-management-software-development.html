The inventory is most dominantly associated with the manufacturing industries supplying or importing various parts and products. Inventory Aggregation and Sync Software

We provide inventory management software solutions that program data aggregation platforms and centralized repositories for inventory count and stock transfer solutions. This software syncs inventory across multiple channels, warehouses, and locations to eliminate redundancies and double-entry errors.

Warehouse Inventory Software Solutions

Afluex Multiservices LLP provides warehouse inventory management software solutions for seamless stock adjustments, warehouse management, stock status updates, channel specific stocking, and more. Our warehouse inventory software services also include development of features.

Inventory Software System Services

We create analytics platforms to report historical sales performance per item. This creates inventory management forecasting modules that generate Economic Order Quantities (EOQ). We develop real-time reporting dashboards to track critical KPIs, including Cost of Goods Sold (COGS), turnover rates, and net profits.

Inventory Management Software Integration

Our expert team of programmers at Afluex Multiservices LLP integrates inventory management software with ERP systems through API development. We also integrate with shelf life management modules with RFID, barcodes and QR code scanners, and POS and e-commerce systems for real-time stock information. Finally, we integrate with document management platforms for tracking receipts, purchase orders, invoices, packing slips, and bills of lading.
